Current Job Openings

Superintendent / Principal

Gardiner Schools is seeking a Principal/Superintendent beginning July 1, 2025. Gardiner is located at the north entrance of Yellowstone and serves 150 students. Applicants with a Principal certificate willing to enroll in a Superintendent program under a provisional license are welcome to apply.

Benefits: Salary based on experience; subsidized housing available.

Application Packet Must Include:

  • Letter of application/interest
  • Completed district application form
  • Resume
  • Three letters of reference
  • Transcripts
  • Copy of Class III MT Administrative Certificate (or equivalent)

Business Manager / District Clerk

Gardiner Public Schools is hiring a Business Manager/District Clerk to oversee financial operations and administrative support. This position may be hybrid with partial remote work.

Requirements: Degree in Business/Accounting, prior school clerk experience preferred.

Housing: Full-time subsidized or part-time available.

Application Packet Must Include:

  • Letter of application/interest
  • Completed district application form
  • Resume
  • Three letters of reference

Bus Drivers (Route, Substitute, and Activities)

We are hiring route and activities bus drivers for the 2024–25 school year, with potential regular routes for 2025–26.

Requirements: Background check, drug testing, and a Class B license with a school bus endorsement (training provided).

Apply using the general application or contact jbray@gardiner.org.

Custodian

Gardiner Public Schools is seeking a full-time custodian. Salary based on experience. Benefits included.

Apply at the school (510 Stone St, Gardiner, MT) or email jbray@gardiner.org.
