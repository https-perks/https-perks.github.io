Our Community


Gardiner, Montana is an unincorporated town on the northern boundary of Yellowstone National Park and is the world’s first gateway community to a national park. Officially founded in 1880, Gardiner has served visitors since Yellowstone’s creation in 1872. The town is nestled in the Upper Yellowstone Valley, surrounded by national park and forest lands, and the Yellowstone River flows through its center. The community is known for its friendly, supportive nature and rich heritage.
